About Us

Kgakullo Galactic Development is a regulatory technology company dedicated to providing intelligent compliance management solutions to make financial markets more robust, secure, and efficient. Our flagship product, Magniifii, is an all-in-one verification platform designed to catch fraudsters and aid businesses in meeting compliance regulations worldwide.

Magniifii.

Magniifii offers a suite of intelligent compliance management solutions that provide complete coverage to our clients. Our products and services include:


  • User verification: Easily onboard new clients and perform AML screening.
  • Business verification: Verify businesses and comply with due diligence regulations.
  • Transaction monitoring: Monitor transactions and detect fraudulent activity in real-time.
  • Case management: Manage cases and report suspicious activity to regulatory authorities.

Magniifii's Features

Magniifii's features and modules are designed to address the challenges faced by financial service providers in complying with AML regulations. Our solutions offer the following benefits to our clients:


  • Reduced cost: Our consolidated process reduces the cost of AML compliance for financial service providers.
  • Reduced processing time: Our platform automates the verification process, reducing processing time and improving efficiency.
  • Consolidated process: Our platform is an all-in-one solution that provides complete coverage, eliminating the need for multiple verification tools.
  • Complete coverage: Our solutions cover the entire customer lifecycle, providing a comprehensive approach to AML compliance.
